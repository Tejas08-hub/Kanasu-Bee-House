Dual-Stage Disease Diagnosis

1

Acoustic Prediction

Our ML model constantly monitors hive sounds. It detects invisible stress and early-stage disease signatures before any physical symptoms appear.

2

Visual Verification

Once the sound analysis recommends a check-up, beekeepers take a photo of the brood or bees. Our second-tier Image ML model verifies the specific disease (like Varroa or AFB).
