What is Beekeeping?

Beekeeping (Apiculture) is the scientific management of honey bee colonies for honey production, pollination services, and ecological sustainability. Bees are critical pollinators influencing global agriculture and biodiversity.

Who Can Adopt Beekeeping?

  • Farmers seeking improved yield
  • Rural youth & agri-entrepreneurs
  • Women self-help groups
  • Small land owners
  • Students & researchers

Advantages in Farming

  • 30–40% crop yield increase
  • Better fruit & seed quality
  • Additional honey & wax income
  • Improved biodiversity

Importance in Food Security

Nearly 75% of global food crops depend on pollination. Protecting bee colonies ensures stable food production, stronger rural economies, and ecological balance.
